About Us

CompuNents Builder offers both goods and services for the gamers in gaming and live streaming industry. As our customers buy gaming PC set or components in our shop, our company also offers a PC building that will ensure the durability and quality of their PC. With means of entering our shop emptyhanded, CompuNents Builder wants their customers to leave with PC components or a PC set in their hands as we offer a one-stop solution for them. Our company also helps our customers to be more knowledgeable about PC building and PC component, we will offer a free tutorial, lessons or tips in building a PC which assures our customers about the confidence we have for our goods and services.



Our Mission

CompuNents Builder aims to help the gamers in providing them durable, efficient and quality computer components. As ICT students, the goal of our company is to ultimately serve the gaming industry with our goods and services.



Our Vision

Our vision is to build and secure a leading position in the expanding ICT industry specializing in gaming parts and PC building by providing best of our products and services in our customers to meet their standards and have a contribution to the new society of technology.
